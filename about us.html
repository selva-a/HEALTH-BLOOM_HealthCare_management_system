Health Bloom care groups


Health Bloom Group is a health care group in India. Founded by selva, the group is based in bangalore and has more than 170 eye care centers in India and 27 care centers across 4 states in South India. Company also operates two multi-speciality hospitals in Trichy. Health Bloom Care centers are day-care centres for treating every kind of patient. They started their first care center in kerala, 2010 at Kozhikode. The corporate office of the hospital is located in Chennai,bangloroe,mumbai. A healthcare journey that began with one pharmacy seven decades ago, we have grown into one of the largest network of health care center providers today. Health Bloom Care network is a premier institution of eye care hospitals in the country and we have been making a difference to over 6 million patients every year.
